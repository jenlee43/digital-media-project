Why was this website created?

Sub-Saharan Mindanao website was created in response to the controversial remarks of a political analyst, Richard Heydarian, comparing Mindanao’s Human Development Index (HDI) to sub-Saharan Africa. Watch his video here.

He made it sound like Mindanao is one of the poorest regions in the Philippines.

As someone who was born and raised in Davao City, Mindanao, I strongly disagree with what he said. It upset many Mindanaoans, myself included.

While there are certain places in Mindanao that are still behind, especially on remote areas, labeling the entire region as one of the poorest regions is insulting and unfair, not only to Mindanaoans but also to the people of sub-Saharan Africa.

Through this website, I want to showcase the real Mindanao—its natural beauty, rich culture, strong people, and why it’s known as the Land of Promise.

Mindanao deserves to be seen, heard, and respected.
