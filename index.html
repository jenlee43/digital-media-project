MINDANAO'S ABUNDANCE

FEATURES

Geography & Natural Wonders

Mount Apo is the highest mountain in the Philippines (2,954 meters above sea level)

Adventure & Water Sports Tourism

Siargao is known as the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines", attracting surfers from all over the world.

Tourist Attractions & Eco-Tourism Destinations

Siargao Island, located in Surigao del Norte, Mindanao, is one of the top tourist destinations in the Philippines.

Economic & Industrial Hubs

Davao City is the largest city in Mindanao, a major trade and business center.

Culture & Indigenous Tribes

Kadayawan is an annual festival celebrating the indigenous heritage and the island's abundance.

Religious & Cultural Landmarks

Built by Samsudin Dimaukom, the former mayor of Datu Saudi Ampatuan, as a symbol of peace and unity.

Agriculture & Natural Resources

Mindanao is a major producer of bananas, pineapples, coconuts, cacao, and coffee.

Wide Variety of Tropical Fruits

Mindanao is known as the food basket of the Philippines.

Unique Wildlife

Tarsier is one of the world's smallest primates, found in Surigao del Norte.
